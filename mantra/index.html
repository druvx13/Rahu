Sacred Mantra

Ancient Wisdom for Modern Seekers

Original Sanskrit

ॐ वाङ् मे मनसि प्रतिष्ठिता मनो मे वाचि प्रतिष्ठितम् माविरावीर्म एधि । वेदस्य म आणीस्थः । श्रुतं मे मा प्रहासीरनेनाधीते- नाहोरात्रान्सन्दधाम्यृतं वदिष्यामि सत्यं वदिष्यामि ॥ तत् मामवतु तद्वक्तारमवतु अवतु मामवतु वक्तारमवतु वक्तारम् ॥
ॐ

English Translation

Om! May my speech be established in my mind; may my mind be established in my speech! Reveal Yourself unto me; shine forth for my vision!

May what I have studied not leave me! May I join, day and night, in that study! I speak the Truth; I speak what is beneficial!

May That protect me! May That protect the speaker! May That protect me! May That protect the speaker!

Essence of the Mantra

This sacred chant from the Vedas emphasizes the profound unity between thought (mind) and expression (speech). It is a prayer for divine protection and guidance in the pursuit of wisdom, seeking a harmonious alignment between our inner consciousness and outer words.

Mind & Speech Union

Seeking harmony between thought and expression

Retention of Wisdom

Preservation of studied knowledge over time

"May our words be rooted in truth, and our minds in wisdom"

May this sacred mantra bring peace, wisdom, and spiritual awakening to all who recite it.
