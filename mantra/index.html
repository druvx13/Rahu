Sacred Mantra

Original Sanskrit

ॐ वाङ् मे मनसि प्रतिष्ठिता मनो मे वाचि प्रतिष्ठितम् माविरावीर्म एधि । वेदस्य म आणीस्थः । श्रुतं मे मा प्रहासीरनेनाधीते- नाहोरात्रान्सन्दधाम्यृतं वदिष्यामि सत्यं वदिष्यामि ॥ तत् मामवतु तद्वक्तारमवतु अवतु मामवतु वक्तारमवतु वक्तारम् ॥
• • •

English Translation

Om! May my speech be established in my mind; may my mind be established in my speech! Reveal Yourself unto me; shine forth for my vision! May what I have studied not leave me! May I join, day and night, in that study! I speak the Truth; I speak what is beneficial! May That protect me! May That protect the speaker! May That protect me! May That protect the speaker!

Essence of the Mantra

This sacred chant emphasizes the divine connection between speech and mind, seeking guidance and protection in the pursuit of truth and wisdom through learning. It represents a profound meditation on knowledge, consciousness, and spiritual awakening.

May this sacred mantra bring peace and wisdom to all who recite it.
