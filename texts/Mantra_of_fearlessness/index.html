मन्त्रः स्वच्छन्दतायै

Mantra of Fearlessness

Vedic Hymn for Inner Strength and Stability

Sacred Invocation

ॐ भद्रं कर्णेभिः शृणुयाम देवाः ।
भद्रं पश्येमाक्षभिर्यजत्राः ।

Translation

Om. May we hear auspicious words with our ears, O Gods.
May we see auspicious things with our eyes, O worthy of worship.

१

Verse 1

यथा॒ द्यौश्च॑ पृथि॒वी च॒ न बि॑भी॒तो न रिष्य॑तः।
ए॒वा मे॑ प्राण॒ मा बि॑भेः ॥

Just as the sky and earth do not tremble or waver,
may my life not tremble or waver thus.

२

Verse 2

यथाह॑श्च॒ रात्री॑ च॒ न बि॑भी॒तो न रिष्य॑तः।
ए॒वा मे॑ प्राण॒ मा बि॑भेः ॥

Just as day and night do not tremble or waver,
may my life not tremble or waver thus.

३

Verse 3

यथा॒ सूर्य॑श्च च॒न्द्रश्च॒ न बि॑भी॒तो न रिष्य॑तः।
ए॒वा मे॑ प्राण॒ मा बि॑भेः ॥

Just as the sun and moon do not tremble or waver,
may my life not tremble or waver thus.

४

Verse 4

यथा॒ ब्रह्म॑ च क्ष॒त्रं च॒ न बि॑भी॒तो न रिष्य॑तः।
ए॒वा मे॑ प्राण॒ मा बि॑भेः ॥

Just as brahman and kshatriya do not tremble or waver,
may my life not tremble or waver thus.

५

Verse 5

यथा॑ स॒त्यं चानृ॑तं च॒ न बि॑भी॒तो न रिष्य॑तः।
ए॒वा मे॑ प्राण॒ मा बि॑भेः ॥

Just as truth and untruth do not tremble or waver,
may my life not tremble or waver thus.

६

Verse 6

यथा॑ भू॒तं च॒ भव्यं॑ च॒ न बि॑भी॒तो न रिष्य॑तः।
ए॒वा मे॑ प्राण॒ मा बि॑भेः ॥

Just as what is and what will be do not tremble or waver,
may my life not tremble or waver thus.

॥ इति शान्तिमन्त्रः सम्पूर्णः ॥

Thus concludes this timeless mantra for fearlessness and stability.

Vedic Chant for Inner Strength • May it bring peace and protection

ॐ
