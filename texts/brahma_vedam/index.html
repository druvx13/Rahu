ब्रह्मवेदम्

Sacred Verse from Ancient Wisdom

ब्रह्मवेदममृतं पुरस्ताद्ब्रह्म पश्चाद्ब्रह्म दक्षिणतश्चोत्तरेण ।
अधश्चोर्ध्वं च प्रसृतं ब्रह्मैवेदं विश्वमिदं वरिष्ठम् ॥ ११ ॥
Immortal is the Brahman: before is Brahman, behind is Brahman; from the south and from the north is Brahman; extending below and above, this entire universe is none other than Brahman—the most ancient and supreme.

अखिलानन्दस्वरूपम् - Embodiment of complete bliss
