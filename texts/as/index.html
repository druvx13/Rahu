Sacred Invocation

चित्स्वरूपां मायामयीं विश्वविलासिनीं स्वप्रकाशां नित्यां आनन्दाङ्गीं असङ्गाम् ईश्वरजीवभेदभासिनीं परां धाम् तां त्वां वन्दे देवीं कृपाकरां शिवाम्
ॐ

Divine Translation

To the One whose very form is Pure Consciousness,

Who is the embodiment of divine illusion,

The playful enchantress of the entire universe,

Self-luminous, eternal, and ever-blissful,

Unattached, yet manifesting the apparent distinction

Between the Lord and the individual soul,

The supreme abode of divine grace,

To You, O Goddess of compassion, the auspicious one,

I offer my humble salutations.
