Devi Stotra

A sacred invocation to the Divine Mother, source of all consciousness and bliss

Original Sanskrit

चित्स्वरूपां मायामयीं विश्वविलासिनीं

स्वप्रकाशां नित्यां आनन्दाङ्गीं असङ्गाम्

ईश्वरजीवभेदभासिनीं परां धाम्

तां त्वां वन्दे देवीं कृपाकरां शिवाम्

Perfect English Translation

I bow to you, O Goddess, whose very essence is pure consciousness, the embodiment of all-pervading illusion that makes the universe dance in delight.

You are self-luminous, ever-present, eternal, your very limbs made of bliss, yet completely unattached to any form.

You are the supreme abode, O Divine Mother, in whom the apparent distinction between the Supreme Lord and individual souls merely appears like a reflection in a mirror.

To you alone I offer my salutations, O compassionate one, bestower of all grace, the most auspicious one.
