Illusory Vision

“जो कुछ भी यहाँ नज़र आता है, उसे ग़ैर मौजूद समझ़ो, जैसा कि शहर-ए-ख़याल होता है, जैसा कि सराब में पानी होता है।”
"Consider whatever appears here as non-existent, like a city of imagination, like water in a mirage."
“
”

This profound verse reminds us of the illusory nature of perception. What appears before our eyes may simply be a trick of the mind, a phantom city of thought, as fleeting as water in a desert mirage.
