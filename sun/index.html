Sun Hymn

Suryakavacha Stotram

Divine Armor of Light - Composed by Sage Yajnavalkya

Invocation

श्रीगणेशाय नमः ।
Salutations to Lord Ganesha

Verse 1

याज्ञवल्क्य उवाच ।
शृणुष्व मुनिशार्दूल सूर्यस्य कवचं शुभम् ।
शरीरारोग्यदं दिव्यं सर्वसौभाग्यदायकम् ॥ १॥
Yajnavalkya said:
O best among sages, listen to this auspicious armor of Surya.
It grants health to the body, is divine, and bestows all prosperity and good fortune.

Verse 2

देदीप्यमानमुकुटं स्फुरन्मकरकुण्डलम् ।
ध्यात्वा सहस्रकिरणं स्तोत्रमेतदुदीरयेत् ॥ २॥
Meditating upon the Sun with his shining crown and sparkling makara earrings,
One should recite this hymn to the thousand-rayed Lord.

Verse 3

शिरो मे भास्करः पातु ललाटं मेऽमितद्युतिः ।
नेत्रे दिनमणिः पातु श्रवणे वासरेश्वरः ॥ ३॥
May Bhaskara protect my head;
May the one of immeasurable radiance protect my forehead;
May Dinamani (jewel of the day) protect my eyes;
May the Lord of the day protect my ears.

Verse 4

घ्राणं घर्मघृणिः पातु वदनं वेदवाहनः ।
जिह्वां मे मानदः पातु कण्ठं मे सुरवन्दितः ॥ ४॥
May Gharmagrni (the heat-rayed) protect my nose;
May Vedavahana (the bearer of the Vedas) protect my face;
May Manada (the bestower of honor) protect my tongue;
May the one worshipped by the Devas protect my throat.

Verse 5

स्कन्धौ प्रभाकरः पातु वक्षः पातु जनप्रियः ।
पातु पादौ द्वादशात्मा सर्वाङ्गं सकलेश्वरः ॥ ५॥
May Prabhakara (the illuminator) protect my shoulders;
May Janapriya (the beloved of people) protect my chest;
May the twelve-fold Lord protect my feet;
May the Lord of all protect my entire body.

Verse 6

सूर्यरक्षात्मकं स्तोत्रं लिखित्वा भूर्जपत्रके ।
दधाति यः करे तस्य वशगाः सर्वसिद्धयः ॥ ६॥
One who writes this protective hymn of the Sun on birch bark,
And wears it on the hand, all accomplishments will come under his control.

Verse 7

सुस्नातो यो जपेत्सम्यग्योऽधीते स्वस्थमानसः ।
स रोगमुक्तो दीर्घायुः सुखं पुष्टिं च विन्दति ॥ ७॥
One who, after bathing, recites this hymn properly,
Or studies it with a tranquil mind,
Becomes free from diseases, attains long life,
And gains happiness and nourishment.
॥ इति श्रीमद्याज्ञवल्क्यमुनिविरचितं सूर्यकवचस्तोत्रं सम्पूर्णम् ॥
Thus completes the Suryakavacha Stotram composed by the venerable sage Yajnavalkya.

Sacred Hymn to Surya, the Divine Sun God - A Spiritual Armor of Light

May the Light of Wisdom Illuminate Your Path
